# University Projects

Welcome to my repository of university projects developed between 2019 and 2023 at the University of Padua and the "Sapienza" University of Rome. This collection includes theses, research reports, and various course projects focused on data analysis, statistics, and computer science.

## üéì Theses

### [Real Estate Market Forecasting: Madrid Case Study](./theses/madrid-real-estate-forecasting)
**Master's Thesis | R, OpenStreetMap | March 2023**
- Compared traditional and spatial predictive models to estimate housing prices in Madrid.
- Addressed missing data challenges through imputation techniques.
- Leveraged high-quality data from Spanish real estate portals, including precise geolocation.

### [Migration and Population Dynamics in Italian Provinces](./theses/migration-population-dynamics.pdf)
**Bachelor's Thesis | R | October 2020**
- Analyzed the impact of migration on population structure (age, gender, fertility) in Italy.
- Applied linear and generalized linear regression models to demographic data.
- Explored the theoretical differences between natural population changes and migration flows.

## üìö Coursework

### [NBA Games Database Construction](./coursework/nba-database-construction)
**SQL (MySQL), NoSQL (MongoDB) | May 2022**
- Designed and implemented both relational and non-relational databases for NBA data (2004-2021).
- Performed query optimization and comparison between SQL and NoSQL approaches.

### [Parallel Clustering on Molecular Networks](./coursework/parallel-molecular-clustering)
**Java, Apache Spark | January 2022**
- Implemented the Girvan-Newman algorithm for clustering Protein-Protein Interaction (PPI) networks.
- Developed using a Map-Reduce approach within the Spark framework.

### [Robust Clustering on Italian Provinces](./coursework/robust-clustering-italy)
**R | December 2021**
- Compared robust (`tclust`) and non-robust (`mclust`) Gaussian mixture models.
- Conducted evaluations through theoretical analysis, simulations, and an ISTAT demographic case study.

### [Text Mining on Rock Sub-Genres](./coursework/rock-lyrics-text-mining.pdf)
**R | June 2021**
- Analyzed lyrics across various rock sub-genres (Folk, Grunge, Punk, etc.).
- Implemented Sentiment Analysis, Topic Modeling (LDA), and predictive models (Naive Bayes, Random Forest, SVM).

### [Social Mobility Analysis of Italian Graduates](./coursework/social-mobility-analysis.pdf)
**SAS | June 2020**
- Investigated social mobility among 2015 high school graduates using ISTAT data.
- Analyzed the influence of initial social class on career paths and education.

### [Survival Analysis for Covid-19](./coursework/covid19-survival-analysis.pdf)
**R | May 2020**
- Analyzed epidemic trends in Japan, South Korea, and Hong Kong during early 2020.
- Studied virus progression across different age and gender groups.

### [Sampling Plan for Exit Polls](./coursework/exit-poll-sampling.pdf)
**R | June 2019**
- Designed a hypothetical sampling plan for exit-poll surveys for the 2019 European elections in Italy.
